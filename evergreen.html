Evergreen Law Firm

Evergreen Law Firm is a team of lawyers and legal administrators that specializes in car accidents and personal injuries. Evergreen was built with a team of four as part of the BCIT Technical Web Designer program using WordPress, PHP, and SASS. View Website

Design

For this project, a team member and I were responsible for the design and content plan of Evergreen Law Firm. There was quite a bit of content that needed to be added to the website. We decided to go with a clean and minimal design to showcase the structure and the flow of the pages. We wanted to make sure that the website showcased a warm and welcoming experience for potential clients.

Development

Once we were ready to develop the website, we wanted to get the main structure of the website done first. We created an underscores theme, downloaded the proper plugins (Advanced Custom Fields, Contact Form 7, Regenerate Thumbnails, etc.), and moved onto to creating custom post types, taxonomies, and Advanced Custom Fields. From there, each team member was assigned a page to ensure content was output correctly and content was populated before we began styling. We used a custom post type and a Staff taxonomy to display the Lawyers and Administrative Staff on the About page.
