Hive

Hive is a DJ company that was created by three DJs. Individuals are able to book a DJ for a special occasion and purchase tickets to a concert. Hive was built with a team of three as part of the BCIT Technical Web Designer Program using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SASS.

Design

For this project, I was responsible for the design and content plan for Hive. We wanted to create a fun, lively, and charismatic design to showcase the personalities of the DJs. We went for vibrant colours, eye-catching images, and different styles of presenting content.

Development

The first thing we wanted to do was to upload the images, fonts, and colours. We wanted to make sure that we were efficient when coding. It was essential for us to add the font and colours to a variables.scss file. Next step, we started to build out the structure: HTML and SASS. From there, each team member took a different page to work on to ensure we met our deadline.
