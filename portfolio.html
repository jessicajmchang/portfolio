Design

I first built a content strategy on how I envision my portfolio to look like and how I wanted the user to flow through my portfolio. I created wireframes to visualize the structure of each page and later on built mockups. I wanted my portfolio to be light, calm, and welcoming. I wanted to convey my personality so I chose colours that were calming and natural.

Development

For this project, I built out the HTML structure and all of the pages that were going to be in the portfolio. The next step, I wanted to create the SASS structure; I added all of the colours, fonts, font size, and line height for the bulk of the styling. As I categorized the pages as each of their own SASS files, I was excited to bring my portfolio to life!
